Ericksonian Principles in Supervision,
Exclusively for Texas LMFT-S

  • Cost: $75
  • June 29, 2018
  • 8AM-11AM EST / 7AM-10AM CST
  • This workshop explores the utilization of Ericksonian principles in clinical supervision. It focuses on how principles like utilizing whatever the supervisee brings to supervision, orienting supervision toward their goals, building hope and expectancy, and the utilization of other Ericksonian methods that can be useful in Supervision. This is a workshop that Bertram and Rankin delivered at the Texas Association for Marriage and Family Therapy Conference in February and it combines elements of other Ericksonian supervision workshops they provided at the Erickson Foundation's Brief Therapy Conferences and Ericksonian Therapy Congresses.
